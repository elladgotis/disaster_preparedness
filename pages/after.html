After the Disaster

Stay cautious after a disaster — dangers may still exist. Prioritize safety and check on others.

🔎 1. Ensure Safety

  • ✅ Check for injuries; provide first aid.
  • 🚪 Inspect your home for damage before entering.
  • 🚫 Avoid evacuated areas until cleared safe.

📞 2. Stay Informed

  • 📻 Listen to official updates.
  • 📱 Message family to confirm safety.
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Check on vulnerable neighbors.

💧 3. Protect Health

  • 🚰 Boil drinking water.
  • 🥫 Avoid spoiled or contaminated food.
  • 🧼 Maintain hygiene to prevent illness.

🧹 4. Clean Up Safely

  • 🧤 Wear gloves, boots, mask.
  • ⚠️ Watch for sharp debris or hazards.
  • ☣️ Avoid contact with floodwater.

🆘 5. Seek Assistance

  • 🏥 Visit barangay hall or health center if needed.
  • 📄 Report damages to local authorities.
  • 🤝 Keep documents ready for aid and relief.
