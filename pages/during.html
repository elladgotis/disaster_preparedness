During a Disaster

When disaster strikes, your safety depends on your actions. Stay calm and follow these guidelines for different scenarios.

🌀 During Typhoons/Flooding

  • ✅ Stay indoors, away from windows.
  • 📻 Monitor updates from PAGASA and LGU.
  • 🔌 Unplug appliances.
  • 📦 Move valuables to higher ground.
  • 🚨 Evacuate early if instructed.

🌍 During Earthquakes

  • 🧎‍♂️ Drop, Cover, and Hold On.
  • 🚪 Stay indoors until shaking stops.
  • ⛔ Avoid glass and heavy objects.
  • 🏃‍♀️ If outside, move to open space.

🌋 During Volcanic Eruptions

  • 😷 Cover nose and mouth with mask or cloth.
  • 🚪 Close windows and doors to block ash.
  • 📻 Follow PHIVOLCS updates.
  • 🚗 Evacuate if in danger zone.

⚠️ General Tips

  • 📱 Keep phone charged.
  • 🧘 Stay calm, help others.
  • 🚫 Avoid rumors and misinformation.
